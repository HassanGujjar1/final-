Mission

The mission of Air University is to achieve excellence in teaching and research for producing graduates with sound professional knowledge, integrity of character, a keen sense of social responsibility and a passion for lifelong learning. The University shall stand committed to creating an environment conducive for attracting the best students, faculty and supporting staff for contributing to the development of a prosperous, peaceful and enlightened society.

Campuses

Islamabad Campus

The campus is located near the Margalla foothills in the south east corner of sector E-9 (PAF Complex) of Islamabad.

Kamra Campus

The campus is established in close vacinity of Pakistan Aeronautical Complex Attock and will have linkeges with Aviation Research Inovation and Development (AVRID)

Kharian Campus

Air University has established its new campus at Kharian which will be fully functional from Spring 2024 Semester.

Multan Campus

Air University Multan was established in 2011 with a noble purpose of serving the region in the field of education. We made a modest beginning with the establishment of two departments, Department of Management Sciences and Department of Computer Science.
